Welcome to MicNoiseGate

MicNoiseGate is a noise suppression tool for your microphone that creates a virtual audio device you can use in any application.

This installer will install:

  • MicNoiseGate.app in /Applications
  • MicNoiseGate Audio Driver in /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/HAL

After installation, a new audio input device called "MicNoiseGate Mic" will appear in your system. Use this as your microphone in Zoom, Discord, Teams, and other apps.

Note: Admin privileges are required to install the audio driver.
